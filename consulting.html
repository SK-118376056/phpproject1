Blockchain:

Blockchain is the essential technology behind crypto currency such as Bitcoin. However with that being said, the potential that this advanced technology contains goes far beyond the likes of Bitcoin. Blockchain connects consumers with suppliers directly removing the need for a middle-man. It is essentially a digital database that tracks the transactions and records them. If we consider other possible uses for this technology then why stop at Bitcoin, it could also be implemented in other exchanges of money, goods and property. Along with that it could eliminate the chance for fraud to occur.

The Immersive Experience:

Immersive technology involves the augmented or virtual reality becoming a major part in areas of people's lives. This includes gaming, advertising in business, in construction, education and much more. Virtual reality is not a knew concept. It was introduced in the 1960's by Ivan Southerland and Bob Sproull, however the device was large and very heavy and needed a mechanical arm to hold it up. It has only been the last few years where VR has really take off. It has become widely popular in gaming and in other industries like medicine, car manufacturing and with architects.
