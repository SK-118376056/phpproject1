My Interest in Tennis, Athletics and Golf

Home-page Interests

Athletic

Every Tuesday and Thursday I go running with UCC's running club. It lasts for an hour of intense and invigorating exercise. During the hour we do a combination of hill runs and laps of UCC's greatest amenities, the running track in the Mardyke Arena. I feel it is very important to get in regular excercise and to push yourself as often as you can.



Tennis

Every Sunday (weather dependent of course) I go to Sunday's Well Tennis Club to play tennis. It is run by UCC and I find it a great way to meet new people. Tennis has always been a passion of mine and I was happy to find out I was able to pick it up again once I arrived at UCC.



Golf

During the summer days I enjoy playing golf with my family. Whether it's pitch & put, the driving range or a simple game of crazy golf, I find it very relaxing and fun while also being able to get out of the house for a couple of hours.
